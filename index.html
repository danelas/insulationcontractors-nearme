Questions

What South Florida homeowners ask us most.

How much does insulation cost in Miami / Broward / Boca?

Most attic projects in our region run $1.50–$4.50 per sq ft installed depending on R-value. Whole-home spray foam runs $3–$7/sq ft and typically pays back in 2–4 years through energy savings. Every quote we send is fixed — no surprises.

Is the estimate really free?

Yes. Free phone estimate, free in-home assessment, and free thermal imaging on qualifying projects. You pay nothing until you accept a written quote and we begin work.

Do you handle FPL and Duke Energy rebates?

Yes — we are a certified installer for both, and we file all rebate and federal 25C tax-credit paperwork on your behalf. Average homeowner recovers $1,200 between rebates and credits.

Is spray foam safe in a humid Florida home?

Closed-cell spray foam is one of the best products for our climate — it acts as insulation and a vapor barrier at the same time, preventing the humid-air intrusion that causes mold. We follow Florida Building Code venting and ignition-barrier requirements on every install.

How long does the install take?

Attic blown-in typically takes 2–4 hours. Whole-house spray foam runs 1–2 days. Crawl space encapsulation is usually 1–3 days. Most homeowners stay in the home while we work.

Do you serve condos, HOAs, and beachfront buildings?

Yes. We carry COI/insurance on file with most Aventura, Sunny Isles, Brickell, Las Olas, Hollywood Beach, and Boca buildings, and can pull any HOA's required documents the same day.
