Why South Florida homes need better insulation

If your home has any of these problems, insulation is the cure.

Sky-high FPL bill

Bills creeping past $300/mo? Poor attic insulation makes your AC run nonstop in the Florida heat. We typically cut summer bills by $80–$220.

FAQ

South Florida insulation, answered honestly.

How much does insulation cost in Fort Lauderdale & Miami?

Most attic projects in South Florida run $1.50–$4.50 per sq ft installed depending on R-value. Whole-home spray foam runs $3–$7/sq ft and typically pays back in 2–4 years via energy savings. Your written quote will be exact — no surprises.

Is the quote really free?

Yes. Free phone estimate, free in-home assessment, free thermal imaging on qualifying jobs. You pay $0 to compare quotes.

Do you handle FPL & Duke Energy rebates?

Yes — we're certified installers for both. We file all rebate and federal 25C tax-credit paperwork on your behalf. Average homeowner recovers $1,200 between rebates and tax credits.

Is spray foam safe in a humid Florida home?

Closed-cell spray foam is one of the best products for our climate — it acts as both insulation AND a vapor barrier, preventing the humid air intrusion that causes mold. We follow Florida Building Code venting and ignition-barrier requirements on every install.

How long does installation take?

Attic blown-in: 2–4 hours. Whole-house spray foam: 1–2 days. Crawl space encapsulation: 1–3 days. Most homeowners can stay in the home during install.

What if I'm not happy with the work?

Every job is backed by our 30-day satisfaction guarantee plus a manufacturer-backed material warranty (typically lifetime on spray foam, 25 years on cellulose). If something's not right, we make it right at no cost.

Do you serve condos and HOAs?

Yes — we have COI/insurance on file with most Aventura, Sunny Isles, Brickell, Las Olas, and Hollywood Beach buildings. We can pull your HOA's required documents same-day.
