Why your home needs better insulation

If your home has any of these problems, insulation is the cure.

Sky-high energy bills

Bills creeping higher every year? Poor attic insulation makes your HVAC run nonstop. Customers typically cut monthly bills by $80–$220.

Hot upstairs, cold downstairs

The classic two-story problem. Sealing the ceiling plane and adding R-49 in the attic levels every floor within hours of install.

Drafts, dust & humidity

Unsealed attics & crawl spaces pull outside air into your home year-round. Air-seal + closed-cell foam stops it cold.

FAQ

Insulation, answered honestly.

How much does insulation cost?

Most attic projects run $1.50–$4.50 per sq ft installed depending on R-value. Whole-home spray foam runs $3–$7/sq ft and typically pays back in 2–4 years via energy savings. Your written quote will be exact — no surprises.

Is the quote really free?

Yes. Free phone estimate, free in-home assessment, free thermal imaging on qualifying jobs. Contractors pay us a small referral fee only when a homeowner books — you pay $0 to compare quotes.

What rebates and tax credits qualify?

The federal Energy Efficient Home Improvement Credit (25C) covers 30% of insulation costs up to $1,200/year. Most utilities add another $200–$1,500 in local rebates. Our contractors file the paperwork for you.

Is spray foam safe?

Yes — closed-cell spray foam acts as both insulation AND a vapor barrier, preventing the air intrusion that causes mold. Every pro in our network follows IECC venting and ignition-barrier requirements on every install.

How long does installation take?

Attic blown-in: 2–4 hours. Whole-house spray foam: 1–2 days. Crawl space encapsulation: 1–3 days. Most homeowners can stay in the home during install.

What if I'm not happy with the contractor?

Every match is backed by our 30-day satisfaction guarantee plus a manufacturer-backed material warranty (typically lifetime on spray foam, 25 years on cellulose). If a job isn't right, we'll connect you with another vetted pro at no extra cost.

Do you serve condos, HOAs, and commercial buildings?

Yes — every contractor in our network carries full COI/liability insurance and can supply HOA documentation same-day. We also handle commercial, multifamily, and industrial projects nationwide.
