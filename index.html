About Insulation Co. of South Florida

"Comfortable homes start in the walls. We treat insulation like the craft it is — one home at a time."

Insulation Co. of South Florida is a family-owned, in-house contractor serving Miami-Dade, Broward, and Boca Raton since 2012. Every project — from a $1,200 attic top-up to a $40,000 whole-home spray foam — is led by a foreman on our payroll, never subcontracted.

We've thermal-imaged over 2,300 homes in our region and know exactly where South Florida builders cut corners. That knowledge gets your AC bill down, the upstairs comfortable, and the humidity outside where it belongs.
